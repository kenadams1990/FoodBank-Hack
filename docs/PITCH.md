# TideLift AI Pitch

**Problem**: Local catch surplus + frozen perishability + zero canning logistics contacts = wasted protein and empty pantries.

**Solution**: Complete agent that (1) forecasts surplus, (2) negotiates discount pricing with fisheries & canneries, (3) books co-pack slots, (4) stages + tracks production, (5) routes finished cans by real neighborhood need.

**Why it wins**: Hits 6 of 7 Alameda themes. Turns "we didn't have contact info for food kitchens" into an expanded opportunity. Makes people better at their jobs; never replaces the dock handshake.

**Demo**: Live surplus → negotiated canning → equity-aware delivery plan in <3 minutes.
